## Background

The Government Legal Department (GLD) is the largest provider of legal services to government, providing legal advice to most government departments and agencies. GLD handles litigation, advisory work, legislative drafting support, and employment law. AI can significantly enhance legal research, document review, case management, and legislative analysis while maintaining the rigorous accuracy standards required in legal practice.

## Project Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Legal Research AI** | NLP-powered case law research, precedent identification, and legal analysis |
| **Litigation Analytics** | ML models for case outcome prediction and litigation risk assessment |
| **Contract Review AI** | Automated contract analysis, clause extraction, and risk identification |
| **Legislative Drafting** | AI-assisted legislative drafting and impact assessment |
| **Document Review** | Large-scale document review and disclosure for litigation using AI |
| **Employment Law AI** | Pattern analysis of employment tribunal cases and HR policy compliance |
